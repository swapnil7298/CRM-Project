Gharpayy CRM MVP Walkthrough
This walkthrough demonstrates the core functionality of the Gharpayy Lead Management System.

Features Overview
1. Lead Capture & Auto-Assignment
Go to the Lead Capture tab. Fill out the form. Upon submission, the system automatically:

Creates a new Lead Profile.
Assigns it to an agent using the Round Robin method.
Places it in the "New Lead" pipeline stage.
2. Sales Pipeline (Kanban)
View all leads in the Pipeline tab.

Ownership: Each lead clearly shows its assigned agent.
Status Tracking: Easily change a lead's stage via the dropdown menu.
Follow-up Indicators: Leads with no activity for >24 hours are automatically flagged with a "Follow-up Needed" badge and a yellow border.
3. Visit Scheduling
Within the Pipeline, click the calendar icon on any lead card.

Select a property from the catalog.
Choose a date and time.
The lead's status automatically updates to "Visit Scheduled".
4. Business Dashboard
Monitor overall performance in the Dashboard.

Metrics: Total leads, visits scheduled, and conversions (Booked).
Pipeline Breakdown: Visual progress bars showing the distribution across stages.
Upcoming Visits: A dedicated list of upcoming property visits with their current status.
Visual Proof
